About Me

I’m currently a Bioengineering PhD candidate at Rice University where I’ve been developing a diagnostic device that leverages microfluidic design, scalable microfabrication processes, and statistical machine learning algorithms in order to identify early signs of oral cancer. The ultimate goal is to empower dentists to make difficult patient referral decisions with more confidence, shifting disease management further upstream where survival rates and prognoses are much more promising.
